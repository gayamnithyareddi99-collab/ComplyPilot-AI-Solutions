Core Compliance Modules

Six Modules. One Complete Compliance Management Platform.

ComplyPilot AI combines compliance automation, control testing, document management, audit readiness, vendor management, and data security into one unified SaaS platform built specifically for UK hospitality SMEs, dark kitchens, and multi-site catering groups.

Module 01

Compliance-Execution Fingerprinting

Captures, benchmarks, and clones the hidden logic of successful allergen and safety management before it is lost to high staff turnover turning expert chef behaviour into reusable workflow standards.

Unlike static checklists, this engine identifies and "fingerprints" the patterns of successful kitchen compliance zero cross-contamination records, prompt corrective actions, precise allergen segregation allowing these expert-level behaviours to be benchmarked and cloned across an entire team or multi-site estate, instantly bringing seasonal and junior staff up to expert standard.
Module 02

Contamination & Hazard Forensics Engine (CFE)

Automatically diagnoses the root causes of compliance quiet failures pencil-whipped paper logs, incomplete cooling records, unchecked storage zones by analysing the delta between food-safety law and executed kitchen actions.

The CFE identifies exactly where a food safety or allergen control chain breaks down, whether due to supplier labelling ambiguity, kitchen layout design flaws, or staff training gaps, and provides actionable corrective optimisations before a critical failure escalates into an EHO rating downgrade or legal liability.
Module 03

Outcome-Learning Memory (OLM)

Generates comprehensive reports linking specific on-the-floor compliance behaviours to Hygiene Ratings (FHRS) and operational margins, giving independent operators multi-unit compliance intelligence.

EHO audit feedback, supplier ingredient adjustments, and local regulatory changes are transformed into "workflow intelligence" using proprietary algorithms that map successful audits and perfect FHRS scores back to the original kitchen staff preparation routines protecting institutional safety memory permanently.
Module 04

Intent-to-Workflow Auto-Synthesis

Maps unstructured kitchen requests onto real-time regulatory and dietary conditions, automatically deconstructing sudden ingredient substitutions into a structured, multi-step preparation workflow.

When suppliers swap ingredients or a complex high-risk allergen profile appears, ComplyPilot AI removes the need for staff to guess allergen content or manually rewrite kitchen cards directly mitigating risk under Natasha's Law without requiring manual managerial intervention.
Module 05

Community Compliance Network (CCN)

Federated learning architecture enabling anonymised "Compliance DNA" sharing across UK hospitality operators so every SME benefits from regional inspection and allergen trend intelligence.

Insights learned from high-purity QSR chains in Birmingham can be immediately deployed to optimise the safety parameters of an independent bistro in London, generating a collective intelligence ecosystem that new market entrants would have to build entirely from scratch.
Module 06

Hardware-Agnostic Integration Suite

Plug-and-play API platform built to sit above any Bluetooth temperature probe, IoT sensor, EPOS/KDS system, or label printer minimising onboarding friction without vendor lock-in.

ComplyPilot AI overlays your existing kitchen and POS tools Square, Toast, Lightspeed ensuring restaurants are never locked into a single physical equipment provider as technologies evolve, and reducing onboarding time from days to hours once integrations are standardised.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About ComplyPilot AI Solutions

What exactly is the "Compliance-Execution Gap" that ComplyPilot AI addresses?

+
The "Compliance-Execution Gap" is the critical operational gap between regulatory registry compliance and actual kitchen-level operational control. While 85% of UK operators are digitising operations, only 29.7% have integrated structured digital food safety systems, leaving over 70% of kitchen teams with inconsistent allergen verification and temperature logging. ComplyPilot AI bridges this gap by acting as a "Real-Time Rules Compiler" between busy kitchen staff and underlying food safety legislation such as Natasha's Law and the FSA's Safer Food, Better Business framework.

How does ComplyPilot AI integrate with our existing kitchen systems?

+
ComplyPilot AI is built with a "Hardware-Agnostic" philosophy our plug-and-play API platform connects seamlessly with dominant UK EPOS/KDS toolsets including Square, Toast, and Lightspeed, plus Bluetooth temperature probes, IoT sensors, and label printers. This minimises onboarding friction and creates deep integration without expensive proprietary hardware investments. The platform sits above your existing tools not as a replacement for them.

What is "Compliance Equity" and why is it important for UK SMEs?

+
Compliance Equity is the proprietary framework by which ComplyPilot AI captures, standardises, and permanently encodes expert-level kitchen safety logic as an organisational asset. Unlike informal know-how held by individual chefs, ComplyPilot AI allows firms to own their "Operational Safety Logic" indefinitely. Even when experienced staff leave during the sector's frequent turnover, expert-level compliance rules remain embedded in the platform instantly accessible to junior and seasonal hires.

How accurate are the hazard forensics and risk predictions?

+
Pilot validation demonstrated strong early performance: 81% of 150 UK hospitality managers rated the hazard forensics prototype as "highly impressive." Pilot deployments across 5 UK kitchen locations showed a 28% average increase in food safety log compliance, an 18% reduction in allergen cross-contamination indicators across 1,200 tested prep cycles, and new kitchen staff reaching full compliance proficiency within 14 days vs. a historical mean of 45 days. 91.2% of 182 respondents described saved workflow cloning as "valuable" or "extremely valuable."

How does the platform help with UK regulatory compliance (FSA, ICO, GDPR)?

+
ComplyPilot AI is strategically designed to align with the UK's stringent food safety and data regulatory landscape specifically Natasha's Law, the FSA's Safer Food Better Business framework, and the ICO's guidance on workplace monitoring and UK GDPR. The platform enables businesses to track, measure, and audit every kitchen interaction with high-fidelity logs via the Contamination & Hazard Forensics Engine, providing a robust, audit-ready trail for EHO inquiries while protecting individual staff privacy.

Is sensitive kitchen data secure? How does the Community Compliance Network (CCN) work?

+
ComplyPilot AI maintains strict adherence to UK GDPR, ICO data protection guidance, and FSA digital modernisation goals. The Community Compliance Network uses federated learning architecture enabling anonymised "Compliance DNA" from UK hospitality operators to continuously improve predictive models without sharing sensitive corporate or client data. All kitchen data is processed by secure cloud-native infrastructure with end-to-end encryption, and all data entering the CCN is fully anonymised before sharing.
